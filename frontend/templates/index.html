ABOUT US

Our mission is straightforward: to support students in their academic journey by providing affordable, quality meals through our app. As students at Northeastern, we understand the financial strains and time constraints of university life firsthand. By partnering with the university's student resources, we're making dining more accessible while fostering a sense of community between students and local businesses. Our goal is to empower students to thrive academically, financially, and personally, one meal at a time.

Ever craved something more flavorful than the dining hall options, without the hefty price tag of takeout? Us too. That's why we created our app – to bring tasty, affordable meals from local restaurants straight to campus.

How it works

Imagine this: you're a hungry student browsing our app, craving a delicious meal from a local restaurant. At the same time, fellow students are using the app, maybe grabbing their own meals or passing by restaurants. They see an opportunity to earn some quick cash by delivering orders nearby. When an order comes in, they swing by the restaurant, grab the meal, and deliver it right to your door. It's a win-win for everyone involved – convenient, tasty meals for students, and a flexible way to earn money for fellow students. That's the magic of our app – bringing our campus community together, one meal at a time.
